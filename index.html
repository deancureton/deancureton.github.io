Hello, I'm Dean

I am a 2022 graduate of The Westminster Schools in Atlanta and will be attending Stanford University in the fall to study computer science and mathematics. Through summer tutoring and enrichment, I hope to share my love of STEM subjects with younger students and help them prepare for the upcoming school year.

Classes I have taken over the course of my academic career include:

  • Mathematics - Algebra, Geometry, Precalculus, Trigonometry, AP Calculus BC, Multivariable Calculus, Linear Algebra, Differential Equations, Applied Combinatorics, and extensive study of Number Theory
  • Computer Science - Web development (HTML/CSS/JavaScript), Python, Java, C#
  • Science - AP Chemistry, AP Biology, AP Physics Mechanics, AP Physics Electricity & Magnetism

Mathematics

One thing I've always disliked about traditional curriculums is the strict adherence to formulaic problem solving. During my study of mathematics, I was taught to apply intuition and big picture thinking that often isn't prioritized in schools. Through tutoring, I hope to introduce younger students to these principles that keep math fun and interesting.

Potential Topics:

  • Pre-Algebra
  • Algebra I
  • Geometry
  • Algebra II
  • Pre-Calculus
  • AP Calculus

Computer Programming

My passion for computer science grew from a childhood love of video games. Driven by a curiosity of how they were made, I began programming simple games, and the satisfaction of bringing my own ideas to fruition fueled my passion even more. After studying computer science for over ten years, I hope to introduce curious students to the world of programming and game development so that they can find their own passion and develop invaluable computer science skills.

Potential Topics:

  • Intro to Scratch
  • Web Design (HTML/CSS/JS)
  • Java Game Development
  • Intro to Python
  • AP Computer Science
  • Music Technology

Science

All too often, students rely on brute force to make their way through science classes, memorizing conceptual information without truly grasping the underlying theory. Whether it's in physics, chemistry, or biology, understanding the fundamentals of scientific calculation and experimentation is key to efficient work, and I hope to be able to improve students' abilities in these topics.

Potential Topics:

  • AP Chemistry
  • AP Biology
  • AP Physics Mechanics
  • AP Physics Electricity & Magnetism

Approach

Through tutoring and enrichment, my goal is for students to improve their work efficiency and develop a passion for the subjects they study. To do this, I hope to introduce students to techniques and principles that help them develop strong problem solving intuition. With these skills, STEM subjects become far less tedious and more natural to learn.

My sessions are intended to prepare students for the upcoming school year by focusing on the big picture concepts underlying their courses, or to introduce new topics to young students.
